## Ongoing Research

**Reproducing ML-based Compound Stability Predictions** 

I am currently reproducing the findings of Bartel et al. (npj Computational Materials, 2020), which critically examines whether ML models that accurately predict formation energy (ΔHf) can also reliably predict thermodynamic stability (ΔHd). My reproduction involves implementing all 6 compositional models (Roost, ElemNet, Meredig, Magpie, AutoMat, ElFrac) on ~154k Materials Project compounds, with a focus on convex hull construction for stability classification.
